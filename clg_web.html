About

Located in Sangareddy, Telangana, our college stands as a premier institution offering both undergraduate and postgraduate programs across diverse disciplines including Science, Commerce, Education, and Management. Affiliated with Osmania University and recognized by regulatory bodies such as the UGC and NCTE, the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence since its establishment. With a sprawling campus equipped with modern infrastructure—smart classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, computer centers, sports facilities, and student hostels—we provide a dynamic and inclusive learning environment. Our dedicated faculty members emphasize research, innovation, and value-based education to nurture future-ready graduates. Admissions to our programs are conducted through competitive entrance exams and merit-based selection, ensuring access to quality education for deserving students. Committed to empowering youth, particularly those from underrepresented communities, the college fosters holistic development, professional skills, and ethical leadership in a rapidly evolving academic and professional landscape.
